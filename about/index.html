Company profile

BETA RELOCATION LIMITED is a private limited company incorporated in England and Wales on 26 June 2026. The company provides London moving and removal services under the trading name Beta Relocation.

What we do

We provide man and van, house removals, office removals, packing, student moves, same-day moves, furniture delivery, piano removals, sofa moving, and difficult-access sofa help where it can be carried out safely.

What we do not do

We do not offer international removals, crate hire, or rubbish removal. Service pages should only describe work we can realistically provide in London and approved nearby areas.

How booking works

Website visitors can contact us by WhatsApp or phone. App booking will become the preferred booking route once the public app is live.

Need help with a London move?

Message Beta Relocation on WhatsApp or call us. Booking and app features will continue to move into the Beta Relocation app as it launches.
