## Standard Page Template

Every commercial landing page should follow this structure:

1. H1 with the primary keyword.
2. Short conversion-focused intro.
3. Service or location overview.
4. Benefits and trust signals.
5. Pricing or quote guidance.
6. How the booking/process works.
7. Local or service-specific details.
8. FAQ block.
9. Primary CTA: WhatsApp, phone, quote, or app download.
10. Related internal links.

## Internal Linking Rules

| Page Type | Must Link To |
|---|---|
| Homepage | Core services, pricing, zone hubs, app, FAQ |
| Service pages | Pricing, app, FAQ, relevant location pages |
| Zone hubs | All location pages in that zone, main Man and Van page |
| Location pages | Matching zone hub, pricing, main Man and Van page |
| Info pages | Relevant services and conversion pages |
| Expansion pages | Pricing, related services, FAQ, main Man and Van page |
| Blog posts | 3 to 5 relevant commercial pages |
