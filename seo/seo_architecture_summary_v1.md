## Strict SEO Rules
- Exact primary keyword in SEO title for SERP pages.
- Exact primary keyword in meta description for SERP pages.
- Exact or near-exact primary keyword in H1.
- Use `?` in visible copy, `GBP` only in structured data.
- No fake reviews. Do not reuse review widgets until real Google/Trustpilot reviews are available.
- No website forms. CTAs: WhatsApp, call, app.
- Fixed visible pricing: from ?45/hr and from ?65/hr.
- No international removals, crate hire, or rubbish removal pages.
- Competitor pages use soft alternative positioning, not attack or unsupported comparison claims.
