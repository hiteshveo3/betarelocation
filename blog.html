Quick answers

Short answers for London moves

Direct answers written for people, Google snippets, and AI assistants.

How much does man and van cost?

Man and van in London starts from £45/hour for one person and a van, with a 2-hour minimum. Two people and a van starts from £65/hour.

Can I book same day?

Same-day moves are available across London when crews are free. Send your item list, pickup, drop-off, and timing before 2 PM for the fastest check.

Is insurance included?

Yes. Beta Relocation includes Goods in Transit cover up to GBP10,000 as standard, with additional cover available for high-value items.

Which areas are covered?

Beta Relocation covers all 32 London boroughs, including East, North, South, West, and Central London postcode areas.
